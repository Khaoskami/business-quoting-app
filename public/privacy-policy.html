Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Summary: Business Quotes App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All data stays on your device, encrypted, under your control.

Who We Are

Business Quotes App is developed by Khaoskami. Contact: github.com/Khaoskami

Data Collection

This application does not collect any personal data. Specifically:

We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any contact information.

We do not collect usage analytics, crash reports, or device information.

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any form of user tracking.

We do not collect location data.

We do not collect financial or payment information (payments are processed entirely by third-party providers such as Stripe or PayFast).

Data Storage

All data you enter into the app (quotes, clients, products, business information) is stored exclusively on your device in your browser's local storage. This data is encrypted with AES-256-GCM encryption before being written to storage.

No data is transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party at any time.

If you clear your browser data or use the "Delete All Data" function in the app's Settings page, all data is permanently deleted.

Third-Party Services

The app itself does not communicate with any third-party services. If you choose to upgrade to a paid plan, you will be directed to a third-party payment processor (Stripe or PayFast) to complete the transaction. Their own privacy policies apply to that interaction.

Data Security

All data stored by the app is encrypted using AES-256-GCM encryption via the Web Crypto API. Encryption keys are session-scoped and are destroyed when you close the browser tab. The app includes XSS sanitisation, URL validation, and Content Security Policy headers.

Children's Privacy

This app is designed for business use by adults. We do not knowingly collect or store any data from children under the age of 13.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to us, you have full control over your data at all times. You can view, modify, or delete all data directly within the app.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent revision.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at github.com/Khaoskami.
